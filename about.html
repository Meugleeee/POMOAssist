How POMOAssist Works

POMOAssist utilizes the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. Here's how it works:

  1. Set Tasks: Start by listing the tasks you need to accomplish.
  2. Set Timer: Set the Pomodoro timer for a specific duration (usually 25 minutes).
  3. Work: Focus on your task until the timer rings, signaling a short break.
  4. Take Breaks: Take a short break (typically 5 minutes) after each Pomodoro session. After completing four Pomodoro sessions, take a longer break (15-30 minutes).
